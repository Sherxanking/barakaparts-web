# Google Play Store - Sozlash Qo'llanmasi

## Yaratilgan Fayllar

1. **PRIVACY_POLICY.md** - Maxfiylik siyosati (O'zbek, Rus, Ingliz tillarida)
2. **privacy-policy.html** - Onlayn hosting uchun HTML versiyasi
3. **DATA_SAFETY_GUIDE.md** - Data Safety bo'limini to'ldirish qo'llanmasi

## Qadam-baqadam Ko'rsatma

### 1. Privacy Policy'ni Onlayn Joylashtirish

Privacy Policy onlayn mavjud bo'lishi kerak. Quyidagi variantlardan birini tanlang:

#### Variant A: GitHub Pages (Bepul)
1. GitHub'da yangi repository yarating
2. `privacy-policy.html` faylini yuklang
3. Settings → Pages → Source: main branch
4. URL: `https://yourusername.github.io/repository-name/privacy-policy.html`

#### Variant B: Google Sites (Bepul va Oson) ⭐ TAVSIYA ETILADI
1. Google Sites'ga kiring: https://sites.google.com
2. "Blank" (Bo'sh) sayt yarating
3. Sayt nomi: "BarakaParts Privacy Policy"
4. `PRIVACY_POLICY.md` faylidagi mazmunni ko'chirib qo'ying
5. Publish → "Anyone with the link" → Publish
6. URL: `https://sites.google.com/view/barakaparts-privacy` (yoki sizning URL)

**Muhim:** Sayt bo'lmasligi muammo emas! Google Sites bepul va oson. Privacy Policy faqat onlayn mavjud bo'lishi kerak.

### 2. Google Play Console'da Sozlash

1. **Play Console** → **Ilovangiz** → **Policy** → **App content**
2. **Privacy Policy** bo'limiga kiriting
3. Privacy Policy URL manzilini kiriting (1-qadamda olingan URL)
4. **Save** tugmasini bosing

### 3. Data Safety Bo'limini To'ldirish

1. **Play Console** → **Ilovangiz** → **Policy** → **Data safety**
2. `DATA_SAFETY_GUIDE.md` faylidagi ko'rsatmalarga amal qiling
3. Asosiy ma'lumotlar:
   - ✅ Data collection: **Yes**
   - ✅ Collected data types: Name, Email, Photos, Device ID
   - ✅ Purpose: App functionality, Account management, Authentication
   - ✅ Privacy Policy URL: Kiriting

### 4. Email Manzilini Yangilash

1. `PRIVACY_POLICY.md` va `privacy-policy.html` fayllarida `[EMAIL MANZILINGIZNI KIRITING]` qismini o'z emailingiz bilan almashtiring
2. Agar Privacy Policy'ni onlayn joylashtirgan bo'lsangiz, u yerda ham yangilang

### 5. Tekshiruv

- [ ] Privacy Policy URL ishlaydi va ochiladi
- [ ] Data Safety bo'limi to'liq to'ldirilgan
- [ ] Barcha ma'lumotlar to'g'ri
- [ ] Privacy Policy'da email manzili to'g'ri kiritilgan

## Muhim Eslatmalar

1. **Sayt bo'lmasligi muammo emas!** Privacy Policy'ni GitHub Pages, Google Sites yoki boshqa bepul hostingda joylashtirishingiz mumkin
2. **Privacy Policy URL** faqat HTTPS protokoli orqali bo'lishi kerak (GitHub Pages va Google Sites avtomatik HTTPS beradi)
3. **Email manzili** Privacy Policy'da ko'rsatilishi kerak - o'z emailingizni kiriting
4. Privacy Policy o'zgargan taqdirda, Play Console'da yangilash kerak
5. Data Safety bo'limi ilovangizning haqiqiy funksiyalariga mos bo'lishi kerak
6. Agar ilova yangi ruxsatlar so'rasa, Privacy Policy va Data Safety'ni yangilash kerak

## Yordam

- Google Play Console yordam: https://support.google.com/googleplay/android-developer
- Privacy Policy shablon: `PRIVACY_POLICY.md`
- Data Safety qo'llanmasi: `DATA_SAFETY_GUIDE.md`

## Keyingi Qadamlar

1. Privacy Policy'ni onlayn joylashtiring
2. Play Console'da Privacy Policy URL'ni kiriting
3. Data Safety bo'limini to'ldiring
4. Barcha ma'lumotlarni tekshiring
5. Ilovani yuklashga tayyor!
